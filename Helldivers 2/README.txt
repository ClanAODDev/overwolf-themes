Instructions:
Extract the HD2 folder to a temporary place.
Open Overwolf
Click the cog wheel in the top right or the down arrow in the top left and click settings.
Click on About on the left.
Click Development options link.
Click Install extension...
Navigate to and open the HD2 folder and select the AOD - Hell Divers 2-1.0.0.0.opk file.
Click Open.
Locate AOD - Hell Divers 2 in the list of extensions and click launch.
